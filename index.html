About the Game

Ni no Kuni: Wrath of the White Witch is a role-playing game created by Level-5 with animation support from Studio Ghibli. You play as Oliver, a kid who travels into a parallel fantasy world to try to fix what he lost in his own world. The game mixes storybook visuals, emotional storytelling, and classic RPG systems (exploration + battles).
